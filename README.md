# New Wheels SQL Business Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the business performance of **New Wheels**, a vehicle resale company that provides an end-to-end platform for buying pre-owned vehicles. The company has observed a decline in sales and customer acquisition due to negative customer feedback and declining ratings.

The goal of this project is to generate **quarterly business insights** using SQL queries so that leadership can monitor the health of the business and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

New Wheels has experienced a consistent decline in sales over the past year. Customer feedback and ratings have negatively impacted the company’s ability to attract new customers.

The CEO requires a **quarterly analytical report** highlighting key metrics to understand:

* Customer satisfaction trends
* Revenue performance
* Order trends
* Shipping performance
* Customer preferences

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset consists of multiple relational tables representing the company’s operations.

### Tables Used

* **customer_t** – Customer details
* **order_t** – Order and transaction information
* **product_t** – Vehicle details
* **shipper_t** – Shipping partner information

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

1. Distribution of customers across states
2. Average customer ratings across quarters
3. Customer dissatisfaction trends over time
4. Top 5 preferred vehicle manufacturers
5. Most preferred vehicle maker by state
6. Quarterly order trends
7. Quarter-over-quarter revenue growth
8. Revenue and order trends by quarter
9. Average discount offered by credit card type
10. Average shipping time per quarter
